A digital playground for art, design, and code—and for how we design certainty in product and experience.

I explore creative technology at the intersection of aesthetics, engineering, and philosophy: generative art, XR/AR, and AI-driven workflows. That same mindset drives my work as **Architect of Certainty**—frameworks like the Certainty Ladder and the Confusion Audit to identify friction before it becomes rework and to turn user hesitation into product confidence.

---

## Features

- **Creative experiments** — Generative art, XR/AR, and AI-driven workflows; experimental utilities, creative scripts, and prototypes
- **Iteration and exploration** — Built with a mindset of playfulness and exploration
- **Product clarity** — Frameworks for designing reassurance at every step: Certainty Ladder (6 rungs), Confusion Audit, 3-Line Clarity Block
- **Bridging tech and delivery** — From complex tech (AI/XR) to strategic product delivery; clarity over volume
