A digital playground for art, design, and code—and for how we design certainty in product and experience.

---

I live at the intersection of aesthetics, engineering, and philosophy. That means generative art, XR/AR, and AI-driven workflows on one side—and on the other, what I call **Architect of Certainty** work: building frameworks (like the Certainty Ladder and Confusion Audit) that find friction before it becomes rework and turn user hesitation into product confidence.

This repo is where both of those things live together.

---

**What you'll find here:**

Creative experiments—generative art, XR/AR prototypes, AI-driven scripts—built with a mindset of playfulness and exploration. Alongside that, frameworks for designing reassurance at every step of a product experience: the Certainty Ladder (6 rungs), Confusion Audit, and 3-Line Clarity Block.

The throughline? Clarity over volume. Whether it's complex tech or strategic delivery, I'm interested in what actually helps people move forward with confidence.
